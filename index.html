Story

The Shattered
World

The once-stable realm of Zelyanor now teeters on the edge of oblivion. Ancient seals that once preserved the cosmic order have shattered, allowing malevolent forces to pour into reality like ink into water.

You stand as the last hope, a chosen warrior who must traverse these corrupted lands. Battle through beautifully crafted ruins, confront twisted creatures born from the void, and piece together the truth behind this cataclysmic event.
